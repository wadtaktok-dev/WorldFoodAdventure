About the Game

Discover the world one dish at a time

WorldFood Adventure is a colorful educational adventure game for Android. Players travel through different countries, discover traditional meals and complete exciting food-themed challenges.

Every destination combines entertainment, culture and learning. Players can unlock countries, build a food collection, earn rewards and track their progress.

🧭

Your Adventure

  • Explore countries and cultures
  • Complete missions and boss challenges
  • Collect coins, rewards and boosters
  • Build your world food passport
  • Save progress with Firebase Cloud Save

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is WorldFood Adventure?

It is a colorful Android game that combines world exploration, traditional foods, missions and cultural learning.

When will the game be released?

The game is currently being prepared for closed testing and will be released after successful Google Play review.

Will the game be free?

The initial release is planned as a free Android game.

Does the game require an account?

Players can use guest mode or sign in to save their progress in the cloud.

Does the game work offline?

Core gameplay may work offline, while account and cloud synchronization features require an internet connection.
